## 1. GOOGLE ADSENSE (Easiest to Start)

### Setup Steps:
1. Go to https://www.google.com/adsense
2. Sign up with your Gmail
3. Add site: `https://tanners-tech-news.dyadlabs.us`
4. Place verification code in `<head>` of site
5. Wait 1-2 weeks for approval
6. Add ad units to:
   - Sidebar (300x250)
   - Between articles (728x90)
   - Sticky footer (320x50 mobile)

### Expected Revenue:
- 1,000 visitors/month = ~$10-30
- 10,000 visitors/month = ~$100-300
- 50,000 visitors/month = ~$500-1500
